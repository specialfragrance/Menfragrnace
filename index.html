The Master Perfumer

Francis Kurkdjian is one of the world's most celebrated perfumers. Born in 1969, he created his first fragrance at the age of 25 - the iconic Jean Paul Gaultier Le Male, which revolutionized masculine perfumery.

In 2009, he founded Maison Francis Kurkdjian with the vision of creating contemporary fragrances that embody the French art of living. His creations are characterized by their elegance, sophistication, and timeless appeal.

From the legendary Baccarat Rouge 540 to the luminous Aqua Universalis, each fragrance tells a story of luxury, craftsmanship, and olfactory excellence.

THE ICON

Baccarat Rouge 540

Created in 2015 to celebrate the 250th anniversary of the Baccarat crystal house, this fragrance has become a modern classic. Its unique blend of jasmine, saffron, and amberwood creates a scent that is both powerful and ethereal.
